Prairie Hunt

In “Prairie Hunt”, players take on the task of gathering blueprints to build a weapon while evading poisonous snakes. While navigating the terrain and accumulating blueprints to obtain a weapon from your pet to eliminate all snakes in the area.

  • Technologies: Unity, C#
  • Features: 3D navigation, timed objectives
  • Play on itch.io
